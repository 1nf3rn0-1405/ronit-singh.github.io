About Me

Greetings, my name is Ronit and I am a senior undergraduate student at NYU Abu Dhabi. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor in Interactive Media and Computer Science.

I'm passionate about automobiles and interested in designing interactive and immersive experiences for cars, particularly in the software aspects of vehicles. While my current expertise includes computer security, app and website development, and graphic design, I'm excited to explore how these skills can be applied to the future of automotive technology.

When I'm not engrossed in writing code, I love exploring and learning about new technologies. I also enjoy indulging in creative pursuits such as art and graphic design. Additionally, I have a keen interest in personal development and often spend my free time reading self-help books or listening to motivational podcasts.
